The Team: Who are we?


We are all students from the MIT Civil and Environmental Engineering Class of 2014, and this is our senior design lab project. After several years of experience focusing in either civil or environmental work, we regrouped in our last semester to build an innovative network project, taking advantage of our diverse experiences. Clairity is built by utilizing our varied technical and qualitative expertise. Students divided into teams of 4-5 students to work on the calibration, coding, communication, logistics, or hardware team.
